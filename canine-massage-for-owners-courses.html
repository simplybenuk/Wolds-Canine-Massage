EMA Canine Massage for Dog Owners (Online)

We offer a comprehensive online course tailored to dog owners who are committed to providing the best care for their beloved pets. Whether you have a family dog, a competitive sporting dog, or a working dog, our course is designed to benefit all.

By mastering our unique combination of massage techniques, you'll learn how to perform pre-exercise or pre-athletic event massages to prepare your dog, as well as post-exercise massages to expel fluids and promote recovery. Additionally, you'll discover how regular maintenance massages can greatly enhance your dog's well-being, whether they participate in sports like agility, enjoy long walks, or are dealing with reduced mobility due to age or arthritis.

Throughout the course, you'll delve into the anatomy, physiology, and psychological aspects of dogs. This deeper understanding will not only improve your pet's performance and overall health but also help prevent injuries and speed up recovery processes.

Moreover, as you learn to massage your own dog, you'll strengthen the bond and trust between you and your loving companion, resulting in a remarkable and rewarding experience for both of you.

In Person Owner Workshops

We offer a 1 day or half-day course designed for dog owners who are dedicated to providing the best care for their own pet. Whether it be a family dog, a competitive sporting dog, a working dog etc - they all benefit!

By learning our unique combination of massage techniques, you will be able perform these before exercise or an athletic event, afterwards to help expel post exercise fluids or simply as a general maintenance massage. All of which massively benefit the dog whether your pet performs sports such as agility, enjoys long walks or is now less mobile due to age or arthritis, massage can help.

You will work through the anatomy & physiology and psychological aspects of the dog, you will discover that massage not only improves performance and well-being but also helps prevent injury and aid recovery. When an owner learns to massage their own dog, it embraces a deeper trust and bonding in the partnership bringing a remarkable experience for both.

This training is available as one-on-one private training or in groups.
