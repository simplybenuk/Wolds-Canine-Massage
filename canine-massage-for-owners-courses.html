Canine Massage for Dog Owners (Online)

Our online canine massage programme is perfect for dedicated dog owners who want to provide confident, compassionate care at home. Whether you share life with a family companion, a sporting superstar, or a hard-working partner, you will gain the knowledge to support their comfort and performance.

We guide you through practical massage routines that can be used before exercise to prepare tissues, after activity to aid recovery, and as part of a regular wellness routine. Alongside hands-on skills, you will explore the key anatomy, physiology, and behavioural cues that help you tailor your approach to each individual dog.

By learning to massage your own dog you will deepen trust, improve wellbeing, and give them the best chance to stay active for longer. To enquire about joining the next intake, please get in touch.

In Person Owner Workshops

We offer a 1 day or half-day course designed for dog owners who are dedicated to providing the best care for their own pet. Whether it be a family dog, a competitive sporting dog, a working dog etc - they all benefit!

By learning our unique combination of massage techniques, you will be able perform these before exercise or an athletic event, afterwards to help expel post exercise fluids or simply as a general maintenance massage. All of which massively benefit the dog whether your pet performs sports such as agility, enjoys long walks or is now less mobile due to age or arthritis, massage can help.

You will work through the anatomy & physiology and psychological aspects of the dog, you will discover that massage not only improves performance and well-being but also helps prevent injury and aid recovery. When an owner learns to massage their own dog, it embraces a deeper trust and bonding in the partnership bringing a remarkable experience for both.

This training is available as one-on-one private training or in groups.
