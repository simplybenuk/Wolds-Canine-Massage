Massage sessions normally take place at the Wolds Canine Massage clinic room, based near Spilsby, providing a relaxing and comfortable environment for your dog. We are also pleased to announce we will now also be providing appointments at James Street Veterinary Centre, based in Louth.

Outside of canine massage Olivia has also worked as a clinical pharmacist working in primary care.
